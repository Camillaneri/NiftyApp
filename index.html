What is NiftyValue

Nifty Value is an application created to help people navigate the universe of cripto art and support them in the discovery and collection of NFTs.

It integrates methods from network science and artificial intelligence to offer three interrelated services:

  • Discover new artworks according to personal taste and market segment
  • Assess the current and predict the future market value of artworks or entire collections
  • Find promising investment opportunities according to different risk profiles

What do we do

NiftyEValue aims at testing the discovery algorithm of NiftyValue to improve the service.

We will ask you to complete tasks, answer some questions and express your opinion.

The information will be used to improve Nifty Value.

How long will it take?

The test you are about to start comprises:

A short survey, to get to know you a little.

The warm-up task, where you will test our recommendation algorithm.

The main task, where you just need to navigate and discover new artworks.

Two final questions, to collect your overall opinion on the application.

Completing the test should take you about 10 minutes, or more if you like it and decide to test it a while longer, Indeed, you are very welcome to spend more time exploring the app along the way.

What about you data?

During your interaction with NiftyEValue, we will collect anonymous data from the survey questions and from your interaction with the app itself. This data helps us to understand what worked and what can instead be improved.

All data will be stored in on a secure Supabase server and will not be shared outside of the NiftyValue team.
