An NFT a.k.a. Non-Fungible Token is a technology that in the last few years has started to catch on in our daily life (and naturally the web). NFTs are deployed on Blockchains, which is a new promising financial tool.
NFTs represent unique digital (or digitized) objects, capable of guaranteeing the authorship of the piece they represent.

Do you have any experience with NFTs?

NFTs have brought together Arts and Finance in a new way, considering the traditional art market, in which creators (especially the ones working with digital art) often do not have any income from secondary market or protection against plagiarism.

NFTs guarantee artists a percentage of secondary market sales as well as giving the acquirer the opportunity to invest big or small capitals in them just as they would with traditional shares.
